Our Story

Since 1985, Gaga Addara Hotels & Resorts has set the standard for luxury hospitality. What began as a single boutique hotel has grown into an internationally recognized brand known for elegance, exceptional service, and unique experiences.

Our philosophy is simple: to anticipate our guests' needs before they even arise. We blend modern amenities with timeless sophistication to ensure every stay is memorable.
